## Big Picture

**Malicious activity** refers to intentional actions designed to **disrupt, damage, steal, or gain unauthorized access** to systems, networks, or data.

* Attacks are **increasing in frequency and sophistication**
* Understanding threats is the **first step to prevention and mitigation**
* Exam focus: **Recognize attack types and their indicators**

* Recognize **symptoms and behaviors (IoCs)** associated with attacks
