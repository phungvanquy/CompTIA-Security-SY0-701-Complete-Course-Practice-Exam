## Major Categories of DoS Attacks

### 1. Flood Attacks

**Overwhelm a system with more traffic than it can handle**

#### a. Ping Flood

* Uses excessive **ICMP echo requests (pings)**

#### b. SYN Flood

## Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS)

### What is DDoS?

A **DDoS attack uses many systems at once** to overwhelm a single target.

* Uses **hundreds or thousands of machines**
* Often controlled as a **botnet**
* Bots (zombies) attack simultaneously

ğŸ“Œ Key difference:

* **DoS** = one attacker
* **DDoS** = many attackers

## Specialized DDoS Attack

### DNS Amplification Attack

A **reflection + amplification attack**

How it works:

1. Attacker sends small DNS requests
2. Uses **spoofed IP address of the victim**
3. DNS servers send **large responses** to victim
4. Bandwidth is amplified
